Our Story

About SajiloKhata

We build practical digital systems so small and medium enterprises in Nepal can run operations with more control and less stress.

Our Mission

To simplify daily business operations for small and medium enterprises through digitization — making the tools that power confidence accessible for every Nepalese business owner.

The Story Behind the Product

For many local businesses, decision-making depends on traditional notebooks, manual billing, and scattered records. That manual process often causes missing entries, delayed stock visibility, and financial confusion.

SajiloKhata was created as a localized alternative tailored to Nepalese retailers and SMEs, so business owners can move from paper dependency to reliable digital workflows.

How We Build

SajiloKhata is built using Agile methodology, with continuous iteration based on real user behavior. This user-centric approach helps us deliver features that solve actual daily operational pain points.

  • Discover: Study real shop workflows and transaction patterns.
  • Build: Deliver focused features in short development cycles.
  • Improve: Refine usability, reliability, and reporting based on feedback.
